# Cursor Growth Case Study - $0 to $2B ARR

## Timeline

| Milestone | Date | Key Metric |
|-----------|------|------------|
| Launch | 2023 | Initial release |
| $100M ARR | Early 2024 | First major milestone |
| $2B Valuation | 2024 | Rapid growth phase |

## Growth Strategy

### 1. Product-Led Growth (PLG)
- Self-serve onboarding
- Clear value demonstration
- Usage-based pricing
- No sales team required initially

### 2. Viral Mechanics
- Code sharing features
- Team collaboration
- Public templates/galleries
- Social proof in product

### 3. Community Building
- Active Discord community
- Template sharing
- User showcases
- Developer relations

## Reusable Primitives

### From Cursor's Success:
1. **Frictionless onboarding** - Get to value in < 2 minutes
2. **In-product viral loops** - Natural sharing mechanisms
3. **Template marketplace** - UGC driving discovery
4. **Transparent pricing** - No hidden costs
5. **Technical credibility** - Built by developers, for developers

### Application to Other Products:
- Identify natural sharing moments
- Build templates/galleries for UGC
- Optimize for team expansion
- Focus on developer/community love first
